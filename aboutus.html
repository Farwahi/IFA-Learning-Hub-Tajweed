About IFA Learning Hub

IFA Learning Hub Tajweed is an educational platform dedicated to helping learners master Tajwīd — the art of Quranic recitation — with accuracy and love for the Qur’an. Our aim is to make learning accessible through interactive chapter-wise quizzes, mock tests, and multilingual support.
